Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the Tripaz app?

Tripaz is designed to help you plan and organize your trips easily. To get started:

  1. Download and install the app from the App Store
  2. Create your account using email or social login
  3. Start creating your first trip by tapping the "+" button
  4. Add destinations, activities, and accommodations to your trip
  5. Share your itinerary with friends or keep it private

How do I create an account?

You can create a Tripaz account in several ways:

  • Sign up with your email address and password
  • Use your Apple ID for quick sign-in
  • Connect with your Google account

All personal information is securely stored according to our privacy policy.

How do in-app purchases work?

Tripaz offers a free version with basic features and a Premium subscription with advanced options:

  • Premium monthly: $3.99/month
  • Premium yearly: $29.99/year (save over 35%)

Premium features include unlimited trip planning, offline access, advanced travel insights, and no advertisements.

All purchases are processed through your Apple ID account and can be managed in your device settings.

How can I recover my password?

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen
  2. Enter the email address associated with your account
  3. Check your email for password reset instructions
  4. Follow the link to create a new password

If you don't receive the email, please check your spam folder or contact our support team.
